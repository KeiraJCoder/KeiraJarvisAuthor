Book 1

Memoirs of a Vampyr's Daughter: Eden

A young adult fiction book, following the story of Sophie, a girl diagnosed with night terrors. But before long, she will realize the night terrors are the least of her problems.

Her sixteenth birthday looms, and she finds herself in a life-or-death race against time to uncover the real secret to her parentage and the prophecy that says she will end the Night World.

Fraught with creatures she never imagined existed, she tries to find her way to the answers, meeting Witches, Werewolves, and Vampyrs along the way.

Will she find her secrets in time? Or will the denizens of the Night World find her first?
