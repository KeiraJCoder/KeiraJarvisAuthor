MEMOIRS OF A VAMPYR’S DAUGHTER: EDEN

Main Protagonist

Sophie Munday

A haunted girl burdened by relentless night terrors and a past she cannot remember.

Sophie Munday

  • Curious
  • Resilient
  • Empathetic
  • Defiant
  • Uncertain
Character Overview

Sophie Munday is a girl caught between two worlds—one she has always known and another she never believed existed. Her life has been defined by mystery, unanswered questions, and relentless nightmares. As she turns 16, everything begins to change, forcing her to confront a past hidden from her.

Her Background

Sophie was raised by her single mother, Jean, who has always been protective to an unusual degree. She was led to believe that her father was a firefighter who died before she could remember him.

Since childhood, she has suffered from terrifying nightmares and strange visions. She has always suspected that there is more to her past than she has been told, but no one will give her answers.

Her Connection to the Prophecy

Sophie learns that powerful figures in the Night World are interested in her for reasons she does not yet understand. Her blood type is rare, and there are whispers of an ancient prophecy that somehow involves her.

She does not know what it means, but she is determined to find out—even if it means stepping into dangerous territory.

Her Abilities

Sophie begins experiencing unexplainable phenomena, things that defy logic.

  • Heightened Awareness: She notices details others miss, as if she is sensing something deeper.
  • Strange Dreams & Visions: Her nightmares begin feeling too real, as if they are showing her something.
  • Unusual Reflexes: In moments of crisis, she reacts with speed and precision beyond a normal human.

She does not yet know what this means, but she refuses to ignore it.

Her Relationships

Jean Munday (Mother)

Jean has kept secrets from Sophie for her entire life, but Sophie begins to sense that something is missing. Their relationship is close, but Jean’s fear for Sophie is undeniable.

The Master (Mysterious Figure)

Sophie becomes aware of a presence watching her, though she does not know who—or what—he is. The whispers in the night suggest that she is part of something much larger than she realizes.

Aeryn (An Unseen Connection)

Sophie begins having strange visions, ones that feel too vivid to be dreams. In them, she is in places she has never been, experiencing things that are not her own memories. She does not yet know who Aeryn is—or why she is seeing through her eyes.

⚠ Spoilers

Discovering the Truth

After years of uncertainty, Sophie finally uncovers the truth— her mother has been lying to her. Her real father is Eden, a man shrouded in mystery and fear—and he is not human.

This revelation sends her on a relentless search for the truth, even when every answer leads to more questions.

Sophie’s Growth & Defiance

Sophie refuses to be a passive player in her own life. Instead of waiting for answers, she takes risks, seeks the truth, and begins forging her own path.

  • She confronts the supernatural head-on, even when it terrifies her.
  • She fights back against forces trying to control her.
  • She begins to question the very nature of her identity.

By the end of Book One, she is not the same girl who started this journey— she is becoming something far greater.

Mysteries & Unanswered Questions
  • What exactly does the prophecy say about her?
  • What is the true source of her visions and abilities?
  • Who is watching her—and what do they want?
  • What will she become if she fully embraces the truth?

Main Characters

Eden

A timeless guardian who lingers in the shadows, bound by secrets and duty.

Eden

  • Brooding & Tormented
  • Strategically Intelligent & Calculating
  • Protective, Yet Distrustful
  • Morally Torn
  • Emotionally Guarded
  • Telepathic & Empathic
  • Powerful, But Haunted
Character Overview

Eden is a Vampyr with a past steeped in darkness. Once The Master's most trusted disciple, he has spent centuries attempting to atone for his past crimes. Now, with his daughter Sophie caught in the Night World’s grasp, he is forced to confront his past head-on.

His History with The Master

Eden was once The Master's protégé, trained in his ways and raised in his court of terror. He committed countless atrocities before finally breaking away. Few who have defied The Master have lived to tell the tale—Eden is one of them.

Though he outwardly severed ties, he maintained the illusion of loyalty for years to keep Sophie hidden.

Sophie’s Birth & His Banishment from Her Life

Eden met Jean Munday in the late 1980s at The Ruskin Arms Pub in East Ham. Their whirlwind romance led to Jean’s pregnancy— something neither of them thought was possible.

When Jean discovered what he truly was, she was horrified. She cut him out of her life and forbade him from seeing Sophie. Despite this, Eden could not stay away, secretly watching over his daughter for years.

His Psychic Abilities & Internal Struggles

Eden possesses enhanced telepathy and empathy, allowing him to sense emotions and sometimes hear thoughts. This ability fuels his guilt, as he can feel the pain of those he has hurt.

His past is soaked in blood, and though he seeks redemption, he believes it is forever out of reach.

The Prophecy & Sophie’s Danger

Eden returned to Sophie’s life to protect her from The Master, who believes she is the key to an ancient prophecy.

The prophecy foretells the “Downfall of Night”, but Eden does not fully understand its meaning. He only knows that The Master collected her blood to confirm she is the prophesied one.

Unsure whether Sophie will be The Master’s downfall or something more dangerous, Eden struggles between protecting her and preparing her for what lies ahead.

Key Relationships

Sophie Munday (Daughter)

Eden never expected to be part of Sophie’s life, but when they finally meet, he feels an overwhelming sense of love and responsibility. He is fiercely protective of her but fears he may not be strong enough to keep her safe from The Master.

Jean Munday (Former Lover)

Jean was the love of his life. Though he understood her decision to push him away, it left him heartbroken. He respected her choice to raise Sophie as human and never tried to take that from her.

Aeryn (A Bond Beyond Words)

Aeryn and Eden share a deep, unspoken bond forged in blood, survival, and shared tragedy. Their past remains largely untold, but Eden clearly feels responsible for her fate.

Though he rarely expresses emotion, his determination to keep her safe speaks volumes about how much she means to him.

Whether out of duty, guilt, or something more, Eden will stop at nothing to free her.

The Master (Enemy & Former Mentor)

The Master is the single most terrifying figure in Eden’s past.

Once his most trusted follower, Eden now actively works against him while avoiding direct confrontation.

⚠ Spoilers

Eden’s True Role in the Prophecy

Eden does not yet fully understand his connection to the prophecy. While The Master is certain Sophie is the key, Eden fears the truth may be far more complicated.

Instead of simply protecting Sophie, he must prepare her —in case she must fight, run, or make a decision that could change the fate of the Night World.

The greatest question in his mind: Is Sophie destined to destroy The Master, or become something even more dangerous?

Aeryn

A Vampyr torn between the past and present, haunted by time and fate.

Aeryn

Elspeth Mary

  • Loyal

  • Noble yet Damaged

Overview

Aeryn is introduced in Book One as one of Eden’s closest allies. She is strong-willed, confident, and dangerous, yet there is a quiet intensity to her that suggests deep, hidden wounds. While most young Vampyrs are drawn to power, Aeryn has always followed her own path, making her an enigma even among her kind.

When we first meet her, she is waiting in a graveyard, impatient for Eden to arrive. Her playful scolding and familiarity with him immediately hint at a long history between them.

Connection to Eden

Aeryn is the only Vampyr Eden truly trusts. They share an unspoken bond—one built on loyalty, survival, and something even deeper. She teases him, but she also respects him in ways that others do not.

She acknowledges that many young female Vampyrs want Eden as their master, but she sees him differently. When he turns to her for help, she doesn’t hesitate, even though it puts her in direct opposition to The Master.

Their connection is complex—one of trust, devotion, and possibly something more.

Knowledge of Sophie

Aeryn is one of the few people Eden confides in about Sophie. The moment he mentions her, she immediately understands the danger of his involvement.

She warns him not to get too close, yet she doesn’t press the issue. This suggests that she knows more than she says—perhaps even about the prophecy or Sophie’s significance.

Vampyr Nature

Aeryn has fully embraced her Vampyr instincts. She is a skilled hunter, stalking her prey with precision and taking pleasure in the kill.

Unlike Eden, she does not hesitate to feed. She is methodical and experienced, ensuring there is no evidence left behind. Her efficiency suggests that she has been living this way for a long time.

She sees the Vampyr world for what it is—a struggle for survival—and while she admires Eden’s restraint, she does not share it.

⚠ Spoilers

Relationship with Eden

After a hunt, Aeryn mistakenly attacks Eden, thinking he is human. He overpowers her easily, but instead of being angry, she laughs— showing that she isn’t afraid of him.

Their tension quickly shifts into something else, and they end up sleeping together. This is the first and only time in Book One that Eden shows a romantic or sexual connection with anyone.

However, Eden immediately regrets it, further highlighting his inner conflict over his emotions.

Defying The Master

When Eden turns to Aeryn for help, she knows the risk. Helping him means betraying The Master, a decision that will turn her into a hunted outcast.

Despite this, she does not hesitate. Her loyalty to Eden outweighs her fear of the consequences.

With a wry smile, she tells him, “Till death do us part, Eden.” This moment cements her complete devotion to him, even if it means losing everything else.

Blaine

A tortured soul bound by his past and his transformation into something otherworldly.

Blaine

  • Mysterious
  • Cunning
  • Charismatic
  • Unpredictable
  • Playful but Dangerous
Character Overview

Blaine is a Vampyr who appears out of nowhere, watching Sophie from the shadows before making himself known. He is playful, teasing, and frustratingly vague, never giving away too much about himself.

There’s an underlying danger to him, but whether he is truly a threat remains uncertain. His motives are unclear, his past a mystery, and his presence raises more questions than it answers.

His Introduction

Sophie first encounters Blaine in a graveyard, where he follows her through the darkness. He doesn’t lunge, doesn’t attack—he simply watches, calculating.

When he finally speaks, his tone is light and amused, as if the situation entertains him. But there is an edge beneath his charm, a predatory awareness that keeps Sophie on guard.

What He Knows

Blaine immediately recognises the name ‘Eden’ and reacts with undeniable shock. He clearly knows more than he lets on, but instead of explaining, he toys with Sophie, keeping his information to himself.

He knows the Night World intimately, but how he fits into it is unknown. He could be a lone wanderer, a scout for something larger, or someone with his own hidden agenda.

His Interest in Sophie

Blaine’s interest in Sophie starts as a hunt—but shifts into something else. He doesn’t immediately see her as prey, and he tests her in ways that aren’t purely Vampyric.

He attempts to influence her mind, but when she resists, he doesn’t get angry. Instead, he smiles, entertained by the challenge.

His Motivations

Blaine offers to help Sophie, but why? Is he being genuine, or does he have his own reasons for leading her down this path?

Why was he even in the graveyard? Was he waiting for Sophie, or was it all a coincidence?

With Blaine, nothing is clear, and nothing is simple.

Jean Munday

  • Protective

Character Overview

Jean Munday is a fiercely protective mother, burdened by a past filled with heartbreak, secrecy, and supernatural mystery. She has spent her life keeping her daughter, Sophie, safe—no matter the cost.

Headstrong and independent, Jean makes difficult choices, even when they break her heart. She struggles with trust, fear, and regret, particularly when it comes to Eden and the world he belongs to.

Her Relationship with Eden

Jean met Eden in the late 1980s at The Ruskin Arms, a well-known rock and metal pub in East Ham, London. She was a rebellious free spirit, drawn to his dangerous aura and undeniable presence.

Their connection was brief but intense, leading to Sophie’s conception— something neither of them believed was possible. When Jean discovered Eden was a Vampyr, she was horrified and immediately cut him out of her life.

Her Decision to Raise Sophie Alone

After leaving Eden, Jean moved back in with her parents for support. She lied about Sophie’s father, telling her daughter that he was a firefighter who died tragically.

Jean refused to let Eden have contact with Sophie, believing it was the only way to keep her safe. Eden, despite this, continued to watch over Sophie in secret, even appearing in her room at night.

When Sophie was six, Eden told her he was her “guardian angel”, but realizing he was getting too close, he disappeared from her life once again.

Jean’s Protective Nature & Conflict with Eden

For sixteen years, Jean shielded Sophie from the truth, raising her as a normal human and denying any connection to the supernatural.

  • She dismissed Sophie’s nightmares and visions as childhood trauma.
  • She fabricated a false past, ensuring Sophie never questioned her origins.
  • She lived in constant fear of Eden finding their daughter.

When Jean found Eden in Sophie’s bedroom, she reacted with sheer panic, immediately calling the police. She knew what Eden was capable of—both great love and terrible violence.

Jean’s Relationship with Sophie
  • Loving but Overprotective – Jean adores Sophie but keeps secrets from her, believing it is for her own good.
  • Denies the Supernatural – She refuses to acknowledge that Sophie’s abilities are real.
  • Keeps Sophie in the Dark – She has hidden Sophie’s true heritage and the existence of her real father.
  • Struggles with Regret – She often questions whether she made the right choice in keeping Eden away.

Character Overview

The Master is a figure of legend and terror, a Vampyr so ancient that his true origins are lost to time. His distorted, creased, and barely human face reflects both his immense power and depraved mind.

Once an ordinary Vampyr, he abandoned all remnants of humanity, amassing centuries of knowledge, influence, and terror. Now, he is the ultimate predator, manipulating history itself to maintain his hold over the Night World.

His Rise to Power

The Master is believed to be a direct descendant of Dracula, his lineage legendary within the Vampyr world. He was sired by Clarke Hinchcliffe, known as The Baron, one of history’s most feared Vampyrs.

His hunger for power drove him to unimaginable lengths, eliminating all who stood in his way. Over time, he became so powerful that even the sun became an inconvenience rather than a death sentence.

With a vast empire of Vampyrs under his control, his reign remains unchallenged, enforced by his reputation for brutality and absolute dominance.

His Obsession with the Prophecy

An ancient prophecy, written in the first century, foretells a cataclysmic event that could destroy or reshape the Night World. The Master has spent centuries trying to interpret and control it.

The prophecy was first sought by Clarke Hinchcliffe, who was deemed mad for his belief in it. Before his death, Clarke passed the prophecy to Malus—some believe The Master killed him for it.

The prophecy speaks of the “Downfall of Night”, a moment in time tied to a specific bloodline. Sophie Munday’s blood has confirmed her connection to this prophecy—The Master now believes she is the key to everything.

⚠ Spoilers

His Powers & Abilities
  • Unmatched Psychic Power – Can sense and control emotions, and possibly read thoughts.
  • Nightmare Projection – Has tormented Sophie with terrifying dreams for years.
  • Immense Strength & Speed – Even among Vampyrs, his power is legendary.
  • Shadow Manipulation – Can appear and disappear in darkness, moving through shadows.
  • Mind Control – Can compel weaker minds to obey without question.
  • Tactical Genius – Spent centuries mastering psychological warfare.
The Master's Obsession

The prophecy’s true meaning remains hidden, even from The Master himself. But one thing is certain—Sophie is the key to everything.

His obsession with control drives him to claim Sophie for himself, ensuring that whatever the prophecy foretells, he is the one who benefits from it.

Seraphyna & Sepherious – The Eternal Gods

  • Bound by Fate

Appearance & Presence

Seraphyna: She glows with molten gold and ember-red hues, her black hair flowing like living smoke, streaked with fire. Her crimson gown flickers with golden sigils, its hem eternally ablaze. Flames coil around her wrists and fingers, casting radiant embers into the air.

Sepherious: Cloaked in shadows, he absorbs light around him, his deep indigo-black coat fraying into mist. His silvered skin glows faintly, appearing both solid and intangible. His long, midnight-black hair moves like a cosmic tide, streaked with spectral blue.

Powers & Essence

Seraphyna: The Goddess of Fire, her presence ignites the air. Her flames do not destroy but are an extension of her soul, responding to her emotions. The space around her distorts with heat, and golden embers swirl in her wake.

Sepherious: The God of Shadows, his essence bends gravity. He absorbs the light near him, and his touch distorts reality. When he reaches for Seraphyna, his form fades into mist before contact can be made.

Fate & Tragic Bond

For eternity, they have been drawn together, yet an invisible force keeps them apart. Their hands stretch toward each other, but fire and shadow can never truly merge. Their love is one of longing, a silent conversation across the void.

⚠ Spoilers

Origins & Rebirth

Seraphyna was once a noblewoman, promised to Count Vannay, but she fell in love with Sepherious, a mere kitchen hand. When Vannay discovered their affair, he had Sepherious executed before Seraphyna’s eyes.

Book 1 – Final Confrontation

In the current timeline, Seraphyna and Sepherious exist as guardians of an ancient vault that Eden seeks to enter. Seraphyna sees him as a threat, refusing to let him through, while Sepherious is conflicted.

Cycle of Rebirth

Their curse ensures they are reborn again and again, always seeking each other but never allowed to remain together.

Character Overview

In Memoirs of a Vampyr’s Daughter: Wisdom, Blaine undergoes a transformation—not just in his actions, but in his very identity. Once a loyal servant of Faye, he begins to question her control and struggles to reclaim his autonomy.

His once mischievous nature is overshadowed by a growing awareness of how deeply Faye has manipulated him. The realization shakes him to his core, forcing him to decide—stay in her grasp, or break free?

Faye’s Hold on Him

Blaine was not just turned by Faye—he was shaped by her. She controlled his thoughts, his emotions, even his desires. For years, he believed his loyalty was his own choice, but in Wisdom, he uncovers the truth: he was never truly free.

He realizes that his feelings, his loyalty, and even his addiction to her approval may have been a result of her influence over him. The moment he begins to resist, he feels the full weight of her power against him.

His Relationship with Sophie

Meeting Sophie changes everything. She sees him as more than just Faye’s pawn, and for the first time, he begins to see himself that way too.

Their dynamic is one of push and pull—he protects her, even when he doesn’t understand why. Sophie makes him realize that he has a choice. And that choice terrifies him.

The Moment of Defiance

Throughout the book, Blaine struggles with his instinct to obey versus his growing need to break free. But when Faye orders him to harm Sophie, he finally makes a choice.

Defying Faye is the hardest thing he has ever done—but it is also the first real decision he makes for himself.

What Comes Next?

By the end of Wisdom, Blaine is free from Faye’s control, but not free from his past. He has no idea who he is without her.

His journey is only beginning—he must now decide what kind of Vampyr he truly wants to be.

Jean is a mother torn between love and the truth she tried to suppress. She spent years hiding Sophie from the supernatural world, convinced that ignorance was the only way to protect her. But in Wisdom, Jean must face the past she ran from and embrace the power she always feared.

Her Hidden Lineage & Shocking Discovery

- Jean is half-Witch, the daughter of a powerful, unknown mother.
- Her latent magic has always been there—subtly influencing fate and reality.
- She swore never to use magic, believing it would bring disaster, yet it may be the only thing that can save Sophie.

Her Search for Sophie

When Sophie disappears, Jean is thrust into a world she spent years avoiding. She follows an ancient mother-daughter bond that leads her into the shadows of London, where she uncovers secrets about Sophie’s true nature. What she finds is worse than she ever imagined—Sophie is changing, her humanity slipping away.

Breaking Her Vows & Using Magic

Left with no choice, Jean turns to the one thing she swore she would never use—her own magic. Jean’s worst fears are confirmed—Sophie is not just special; she is a key figure in an ancient prophecy. Her blood holds the power to tip the balance between war and destruction in the Night World. But what no one expected was that Jean herself would have a role to play in the prophecy.

A Terrible Bargain

To save Sophie, Jean is forced to make a terrible choice. The High Order of Witches offers her help, but the price is steep—her loyalty, her power, or something even worse. She must decide whether she can trust them, or if she is walking straight into a trap.

Embracing Her Power

The magic she once feared is now her weapon, but whether she is using it for good or being consumed by it remains unknown. A tyrant of the Night World, his once-unshakable rule now teeters on the edge of ruin. Yet, his thirst for control remains as insatiable as his hunger for blood.

The Master – Book Two Update

  • Ruthless

  • Obsessed with Power
  • Feared by All

The Unraveling

Once an untouchable force in the Night World, The Master finds himself at a turning point. Whispers of rebellion stir beneath him, and even his most loyal subjects begin to question his rule. His dominance is no longer absolute.

Hunger for Control

Despite his fading influence, The Master refuses to accept decline. He seeks out new allies, resurrects old debts, and strengthens his grip on ancient magick to ensure his reign continues—no matter the cost. His obsession with control blinds him to the crumbling empire beneath his feet.

Powers & Influence

The Master wields forbidden magick that even the most powerful Vampyrs fear. His command over blood rituals allows him to extract power from others, making him nearly immortal. His ability to bend weaker minds to his will ensures that no one betrays him without consequence.

⚠ Spoilers

☠ The Prophecy & Sophie

The Master confirms his long-standing suspicions: Sophie’s bloodline is tied to the prophecy that could end or reshape the Night World. His plans shift—she must either be controlled or destroyed. He sees her not just as a threat, but as a key to unlocking power beyond even his reach.

☠ A Dangerous Betrayal

As his empire crumbles, The Master faces unexpected betrayal from within his own ranks. He is forced to confront the reality that his once-loyal followers now see weakness in him. His desperation forces him to make a brutal decision—one that will forever alter the balance of power.

☠ A Final Gambit

With time running out, The Master devises one last, unthinkable act to reclaim his place at the top. He is willing to sacrifice everything—his followers, his stronghold, even his own soul—to ensure his legacy. But will his past mistakes come back to haunt him before he can see it through? Or has he underestimated those who wish to see him fall?

Faye

A deadly ruler trapped in a child’s body, Faye wields power with chilling authority and cruel delight.

Faye

Otherwise Known As

Faybeth

  • Enigmatic
  • Intelligent
  • Ruthless
  • Charismatic
  • Manipulative
  • Dominant
Character Overview

Faye, originally known as Faybeth, is the commanding leader of a thousand Vampyrs. Turned at fourteen, she maintains a youthful appearance that deceives many into underestimating her. She is intelligent, strategic, and fiercely independent, seeing relationships as power plays rather than emotional connections.

Her Rise to Power

Faye was turned into a Vampyr at a young age, but instead of succumbing to fear, she quickly adapted, learning to control those around her. Her cunning nature and ability to manipulate made her an ideal leader. She built an empire of loyal followers, ensuring their dependence by feeding them blood laced with heroin.

Her Rule & Philosophy

Faye sees weakness as an unforgivable flaw. She commands absolute loyalty and has no patience for betrayal. To her, power is everything, and she will go to any lengths to maintain control over those beneath her.

⚠ Spoilers

Her Conflict with Sophie

Faye sees Sophie as a threat when she realizes her connection to Eden and the prophecy. She immediately calculates the risk and decides that Sophie must be eliminated before she can fulfill her destiny.

Her Downfall

Despite her immense power, Faye is ultimately betrayed. When Blaine, once devoted to her, turns against her to protect Sophie, they engage in a vicious battle. Though she nearly kills him, Blaine overpowers her and drains her of blood. As she weakens, she warns them that Sophie will bring destruction before Blaine personally breaks her neck.

Her Legacy

With her death, her empire collapses, leaving a dangerous power vacuum. The Master sees an opportunity to seize control, and the events that follow alter the course of the Night World forever. Though she ruled through manipulation and addiction, she remains one of the most feared and respected figures in Vampyr history.

The Witches

Hailzy the White

The enigmatic and formidable leader of the High Order of Witches, commanding power that even the Night World fears.

Hailzy the White

  • Commanding & Unpredictable
  • Master of Elemental Magic
  • Mentally Overpowering
  • Respected & Feared
  • Knows More Than She Lets On
  • Charismatic & Manipulative
  • Wielding an Unmatched Presence
Leader of the High Order

As head of the High Order of Witches, Hailzy commands immense power. Her methods are unconventional, her decisions ruthless, and her goals often shrouded in mystery. She enforces absolute loyalty among her coven, and only those who prove themselves are granted her trust.

A Force of Nature

When Hailzy loses her temper, the very air around her crackles with energy. She can summon devastating electrical storms, set fire to her surroundings with a thought, and shatter minds with psychic force. Her control over these abilities makes her nearly untouchable in battle.

Her Appearance

With striking red hair that often falls across her face before she casually sweeps it to the side, Hailzy defies expectations of what a witch should look like. Dressed in a gothic-style t-shirt and black jeans, accessorized with chains and bracelets, she resembles more of a rebellious icon than a traditional spellcaster. However, the raw scent of magic that surrounds her makes it impossible to mistake her for anything but a force of supernatural power.

Abilities & Powers
  • Psychic Attacks: Hailzy can forcefully invade and crush minds with her overwhelming mental power.
  • Elemental Mastery: She can conjure and control fire and electricity, turning rooms into blazing infernos or storm-ridden battlegrounds.
  • Authority Over Witches: As the leader of the High Order, she commands absolute respect, with no room for insubordination.
  • Levitating & Telekinesis: Objects tremble in her presence, and she can hover effortlessly, shifting the space around her with just a thought.

⚠ Spoilers

☠ Sophie & The Prophecy

Hailzy has long known more about Sophie’s role in the prophecy than she has ever admitted. She does not fear Sophie—but she knows that if the prophecy is fulfilled, even witches may not be safe. The knowledge she possesses could either be used to protect Sophie or to destroy her.

☠ The Confrontation

When Sophie refuses to submit to her authority, Hailzy’s rage boils over, nearly triggering a magical catastrophe. Only at the last moment does she regain control—perhaps realizing Sophie is too important to destroy. This moment cements her as both a dangerous enemy and a potential ally.

☠ A Calculated Move

Though outwardly hostile, Hailzy’s true motives remain unclear. Does she seek to protect Sophie, use her, or destroy her? She is not one to act without careful thought, and every encounter with her has layers of unseen intent. Her choices could change the fate of the Night World.

Murwill

A distinguished member of the High Order of Witches, known for her wisdom and composed demeanor.

Murwill

  • Respected High Order Witch
  • Practitioner of Ancient Magick
  • Wise and Observant
  • Advisor to the Coven
  • Master of Protective Spells
Character Overview

Murwill is a prominent member of the High Order of Witches, standing alongside Hailzy the White and Bronwyn. She is a skilled practitioner of magick and plays an important role in guiding the Order's decisions.

Appearance & Personality

Murwill is described as a tall and slim witch with long golden hair. She has a warm and kind demeanor, often smiling and presenting a calming presence. Unlike the more assertive Hailzy, Murwill exudes a quiet authority, choosing her words carefully. She is known for her ability to de-escalate conflicts within the Order and provide sound judgment when chaos looms.

Abilities & Role

As a member of the High Order, Murwill is an expert in ancient magick and ritual work. She is particularly adept at protective spells and enchantments. While Hailzy is the leader of the Order, Murwill acts as an advisor and trusted ally, ensuring balance in their decisions. She is a key strategist in magical warfare, often crafting shields and barriers that keep the Order’s sanctum safe from intrusion.

Notable Appearances

Murwill is first introduced when Aeryn meets the High Order of Witches. She plays an active role in the discussions regarding Aeryn and the prophecy, often exchanging quiet words with Hailzy. Her presence signifies the importance of wisdom and stability in the Order's actions. When Hailzy’s temper flares, it is often Murwill who calms her, ensuring decisions are made with logic rather than emotion.

⚠ Spoilers

☠ The Hidden Knowledge

Murwill is one of the few within the Order who knows the full extent of the prophecy surrounding Sophie. Unlike Hailzy, who is more aggressive in her pursuit of answers, Murwill believes that fate must take its course, and interfering too heavily could bring disaster. However, whether her guidance will be enough to prevent catastrophe remains to be seen.

☠ The Secret Protection

Unbeknownst to most, Murwill has woven powerful protective enchantments around Sophie from afar. She recognizes the potential in her and seeks to ensure she survives long enough to shape her own destiny. However, these protections have their limits, and Murwill fears the moment when Sophie must face the Night World alone.

Bronwyn

A high-ranking member of the High Order of Witches, known for her wisdom and quiet strength.

Bronwyn

  • Respected High Order Witch
  • Master of Ritual Magick
  • Wise & Kind-Hearted
  • Quiet Yet Commanding Presence
  • A Voice of Reason
Character Overview

Bronwyn is a high-ranking member of the High Order of Witches, serving alongside Hailzy the White and Murwill. She is known for her deep wisdom and quiet strength, often acting as a trusted advisor who helps balance the Order’s decisions.

Appearance & Personality

Bronwyn is the larger of the two witches who accompany Hailzy. She has an air of quiet authority, her presence alone enough to command respect. Despite her imposing stature, she is a kind-hearted individual, known for her patience and ability to de-escalate tensions within the Order. Unlike Hailzy, who leads with intensity, or Murwill, who prefers to observe, Bronwyn is the steadying force between them, offering a measured and thoughtful approach to problems.

Abilities & Role

Bronwyn specializes in ritual magick, ensuring the High Order’s spells and enchantments remain potent and unbroken. She is particularly skilled in binding spells and warding magick, making her invaluable when securing the Order’s sanctum. While she follows Hailzy’s leadership, Bronwyn is an integral part of decision-making within the Order, often whispering wisdom into Hailzy’s ear before key moments. Her guidance is sought in matters of both strategy and diplomacy.

Notable Appearances

Bronwyn is first introduced alongside Murwill when Aeryn meets the High Order of Witches. She later plays a role in critical discussions, often seen providing counsel to Hailzy. During moments of conflict, she acts as a mediator, ensuring rash decisions are reconsidered before they become irreversible. Her presence is a sign of stability within the High Order, a reminder that power must be wielded with wisdom.

⚠ Spoilers

☠ The Hidden Balance

Bronwyn plays a more significant role in Aeryn’s journey than initially apparent. While Hailzy’s intentions toward Sophie remain ambiguous, Bronwyn secretly ensures that the balance between forces within the High Order does not shift dangerously in one direction. She knows that if the prophecy unfolds as foretold, they must be prepared—one way or another.

☠ The Silent Protector

Though she remains outwardly neutral, Bronwyn has her own hidden agenda. Unlike Hailzy, who seeks control, and Murwill, who values destiny, Bronwyn believes in preparing for the worst. She has already put countermeasures in place in case the prophecy turns against them. But the question remains—who is she truly protecting?

Powerful Beings

Mischa

The enigmatic and unpredictable outcast, wielding magic beyond comprehension.

Mischa

Otherwise Known As

"The Unfathomable Witch"

  • Mysterious
  • Powerful
  • Detached
  • Unpredictable
  • Outcast
  • Loyal in her own way
Character Overview

Mischa is an enigma—a witch of immense power, feared even by her own kind. Though she is loosely affiliated with a coven, she is considered an outcast due to the sheer magnitude of her abilities. Her speech is often fragmented, making her difficult to understand at first, but those who spend time with her find her words slowly becoming clearer—as if reality itself bends around her. Some whisper that she is not entirely bound by the normal flow of time, that she exists partially in a different plane of existence.

Her Appearance

Mischa is hauntingly thin, yet not grotesquely skeletal. Her long, flowing light blonde hair cascades in soft waves, framing her sharp, delicate features. Her large, wide-set blue eyes possess an unnatural vibrance, as though she is always seeing something beyond what is in front of her. Her skin is pale, almost porcelain, devoid of blemishes, freckles, or any signs of exposure to the elements, adding to her eerie presence. Despite her fragile appearance, she moves with an unnatural grace, almost gliding rather than walking, and she never makes a sound unless she intends to.

Personality & Mannerisms

Mischa is aloof and unpredictable, her motivations often unclear. She speaks in a cryptic manner, rarely giving direct answers, and often seems amused by the confusion she causes. She enjoys observing others, especially when they struggle to comprehend her presence. Her detached nature makes her difficult to befriend, but those who manage to earn her trust find that she is fiercely loyal in her own strange way. Though she rarely shows overt emotion, there are fleeting moments of something deeper—perhaps loneliness, or even sadness, buried beneath her enigmatic exterior.

Her Connection to the Witches

Though technically a member of a coven, Mischa is treated as an outcast. The other witches view her with suspicion and fear, knowing that if she ever turned against them, she could annihilate their entire order. It is whispered that she remains among them not out of loyalty, but because it amuses her. There are rumors that the coven once tried to cast her out completely, but the attempt ended in disaster. None speak of what happened, but the scars of that failure remain within the coven's hierarchy.

Her Unique Abilities

Mischa’s magic is unlike that of any other witch. She does not rely on incantations, rituals, or potions. Instead, reality itself seems to shift in response to her presence. Her abilities include:

  • Perception Warping: People around her begin to hear her words more clearly over time, as if the world itself adjusts to accommodate her presence. Those who spend enough time near her sometimes report seeing glimpses of things that should not exist.
  • Vanishing & Appearing: She moves without sound, appearing and disappearing at will. It is unclear whether she actually teleports or if she simply steps into another plane of existence.
  • Unknowable Knowledge: She seems to know things she should not—as if she can see past, present, and future all at once. When questioned, she rarely explains how she knows what she does, only offering vague smiles or cryptic responses.

⚠ Spoilers

Her Connection to Aeryn

Mischa rescues Aeryn from imprisonment, offering her new clothes and safe passage. Though Aeryn is initially wary, Mischa proves both unpredictable and invaluable. It is unclear whether Mischa acts out of kindness or if she simply enjoys playing with fate. Despite Aeryn’s reservations, Mischa appears to have an interest in her that extends beyond mere curiosity.

The Secret She Knows

Mischa speaks cryptically about an event that has not yet happened. When confronted, she simply says: "It will. You will see." Her words hint at an approaching disaster, yet she seems neither eager nor afraid—it is as if she exists outside the consequences of time. Some believe she is an agent of fate itself, guiding events toward an outcome only she understands.

Her True Purpose

Near the end of Book 2, Mischa vanishes without a trace, leaving behind only a single, cryptic message. The meaning of her departure remains one of the greatest mysteries in the story—was she simply a traveler through time and space, or was she guiding events toward an inevitable conclusion? If she ever returns, it will not be by accident.

Seraphyna & Sepherious

Eternal gods of fire and shadow, bound by fate yet forever kept apart.

Seraphyna & Sepherious – The Eternal Gods

  • Bound by Fate
  • Celestial Beings
  • Fire & Shadow
  • Tragic Lovers
  • Eternal Longing
Appearance & Presence

Seraphyna: She glows with molten gold and ember-red hues, her black hair flowing like living smoke, streaked with fire. Her crimson gown flickers with golden sigils, its hem eternally ablaze. Flames coil around her wrists and fingers, casting radiant embers into the air.

Sepherious: Cloaked in shadows, he absorbs light around him, his deep indigo-black coat fraying into mist. His silvered skin glows faintly, appearing both solid and intangible. His long, midnight-black hair moves like a cosmic tide, streaked with spectral blue.

Powers & Essence

Seraphyna: The Goddess of Fire, her presence ignites the air. Her flames do not destroy but are an extension of her soul, responding to her emotions. The space around her distorts with heat, and golden embers swirl in her wake.

Sepherious: The God of Shadows, his essence bends gravity. He absorbs the light near him, and his touch distorts reality. When he reaches for Seraphyna, his form fades into mist before contact can be made.

Fate & Tragic Bond

For eternity, they have been drawn together, yet an invisible force keeps them apart. Their hands stretch toward each other, but fire and shadow can never truly merge. Their love is one of longing, a silent conversation across the void.

⚠ Spoilers

Book 2 – The Breaking Point

After Seraphyna nearly kills Eden, Sepherious intervenes, forcing her to stop. Eden reveals the full truth of their curse, making them relive their past lives through his words.

Seraphyna is shaken by the memories, and for the first time, she questions whether her rage is truly justified. Sepherious, ever the voice of reason, urges her to consider breaking their cycle rather than continuing it.

The Vault & Their Sacrifice

With Eden granted access to the vaults, Seraphyna and Sepherious face a terrible choice: remain as they are, or sacrifice their physical forms to unlock the truth.

In a final act of defiance against fate, they allow themselves to fade once again, knowing they will forget each other but hoping this time will be different.
